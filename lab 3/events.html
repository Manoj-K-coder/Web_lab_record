Events

1. CampusConnect Hackathon 2025
Build. Innovate. Ship.


CampusConnect Hackathon is a 24-hour team event where students build working prototypes, get mentorship, and present to judges. Open to all majors — bring an idea, form a team, and build something that matters..


2. Vibrance
Annual Cultural Fest


The Annual Cultural Fest Vibrance is one of the most awaited events on campus. It features dance, music, drama and fashion shows. Students from all departments compete and celebrate creativity, culture, and talent.


3. Sports Week
Annual Sport Meet


Sports Week is a celebration of fitness, teamwork, and competitive spirit. Students participate in cricket, football, basketball, volleyball, badminton, athletics, and indoor games. Winners are awarded medals, trophies, and certificates.


4. Welcome Night
Freshers' Day


Freshers' Day welcomes new students to college life. The event includes performances, fun activities, introductions to campus clubs, and an informal interaction with seniors.
