Clubs

1. Coding Club
"Innovate & Code"


The Coding Club brings together students interested in programming, app development, AI, and problem-solving. The club organizes workshops, coding contests, hackathons, and project-building sessions to encourage creativity and technical growth.


2. Photography Club
"Capture the Moment"


The Photography Club is a creative space for students passionate about photography and videography. Members capture campus events, learn editing techniques, and participate in photo walks, exhibitions, and competitions.


3. Sports Club
"Play • Compete • Win"


The Sports Club promotes physical fitness and teamwork by engaging students in various sports such as cricket, football, volleyball, badminton, and athletics. The club hosts tournaments and helps students represent the college in competitions.


4 .Music Club
"Feel the Rhythm"


The Music Club provides a platform for singers, instrumentalists, and music enthusiasts. Members learn, perform, and participate in musical events across campus.
