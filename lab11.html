About Us

Dr. Ambedkar Institute of Technology (Dr. AIT) was founded by M.H. Jayaprakash Narayan in 1980. Named after Dr. B.R. Ambedkar, the institute is affiliated to Visvesvaraya Technological University (VTU), Belagavi and is recognized and accredited by AICTE and NBA respectively. The institution is accredited by NAAC with ‘A+’ Grade. The Institution has achieved 163 Rank in National Institutional Ranking Framework (NIRF). Welcome to the Computer Science and Engineering programme at Dr.Ambedkar Institute of Technology, Bengaluru. The department was established in the year 1987. It presently offers B.E., M.Tech. , MCA and Ph.D. degree programmes.
